Questions & Answers

What is the purpose of this tool?
This tool aims to predict whether a patient has diabetes based on diagnostic measurements, using data from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases.
Who can use this tool?
This tool is designed for healthcare professionals, researchers, and anyone interested in predicting diabetes based on medical data.
How accurate is the prediction?
The accuracy of the prediction depends on the quality and completeness of the input data. Our model is trained on a dataset from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases.
What data is required for the prediction?
The model requires the following inputs: Pregnancies, Glucose, Blood Pressure, Skin Thickness, Insulin, BMI, Diabetes Pedigree Function, and Age.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we prioritize data security and privacy. All data submitted to our tool is handled with strict confidentiality and security measures.
